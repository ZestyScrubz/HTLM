Build A PC

Just a heads up, this is a quick way to build just so you have a vague idea. Building a PC will take a little longer and will be harder than this.

All extra tool you will need is a screwdriver

Step 1

Grab your motherboard and put it on the motherboard box. This prevents the motherboard to get dirty and it's good if you don't have an anti-static work surface.

...

I will be using this mother board to demostrate.

Step 2

Push the lever down and then pull it out, then the retention plate should lift. Carefully take out your CPU out of the box. Align the gold triangle on the CPU with the gold triangle on the motherboard. Gentally place the CPU in the motherboard. **The CPU should slide in**

...

There are pins at the bottom of the CPU and if one of these pin break, the CPU might not work. So just be careful. The CPU should fit in the motherboard without force. You can give your CPU a little wiggle to make sure it is fit in and the close the retention plate.

Step 3

Grab your RAM out of the box and insert it in the RAM slot.

...

Before you put in your RAM, make sure you check for the notch of the RAM in order to fit in the RAM slot. Open both side of the latch on the motherboard and insert it. You might need a little force to fit it in.

Step 4

Take your SSD and insert it in the m.2 slot.

...

You might have to unsrew the plate that covers it like this motherboard. You are going to angle it and insert it in to the hole. Hold down the SSD and scew it in.

Step 5

Take your CPU cooler and screw it on top the the CPU.

...

There is a back plate that you need to put on the back of the motherboard right under the cpu so that you can install the cooler. And screw the cooler in.

Step 6

Put your motherboard with all the parts in your case.

...

There might be case fans you need to install first so do that. Screw the motherboard into you case. Make sure you orient the motherboard so that the port are facing the right of the case.

Step 7/8

Plug all your wiring in to all the component of the PC. Then you insert your graphics card. And wire that up.

...

When inserting the GPU make sure you orient it the right way, and you might need to remove the PCI slot cover.

Step 9

Put in your Hard Drive if you have one.

...

Pull out the hard drive slot and wire it up to the motherboard.

Step 10

Insert your power supply.

...

Wire up you power supply to the motherboard.

Last Step

Add OS

...

You should dowload it on a seprate USB and install it onto your PC.

Finish product
